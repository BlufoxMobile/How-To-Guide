MobileBYOD

Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) Orders

Process a customer's personal device activation in Retail360 — step by step

▼
🛠️
Tool
Retail360
Retail store & CoE only
All others use XM360
⏱️
Billing Start
New: on activation
Existing: immediately on order
📅
Cancel Window
14 Days
No monthly charges if cancelled in time

📋 Step-by-Step

1
Fully authenticate the customer — then log into Retail360 using their 16-digit CSG account number. Choose Driver's License as ID type.
2
Scroll to the bottom → click Launch Mobile. If they already have XM service, their account details load automatically.
3
Make sure you're on the Shop tab (Retail360 sometimes defaults to Care). Enter store info, ID type, DOB, SSN, and number of lines → Submit.
4
Click Continue under Bring Your Own Device. Enter the device IMEI and current carrier.
5
Compatibility check runs. Compatible = you see "Good news, your device is compatible."
Incompatible reasons: wrong tech, device locked, outdated OS, lost/stolen, already on XM.
6
Choose SIM type: eSIM (auto-selected for capable devices) or pSIM (enter the ICCID — typically starts with 89148).
7
Pick data plan → keep or port existing number → enter device nickname → Submit.
8
Review Cart Summary with customer → Continue to Checkout. Enter billing address, credit card, email, contact info.
9
Send Customer Agreement: Try Text first → Email if text fails → Verbal/DocuSign if both fail.
10
Read T&C script verbatim → get agreement → click Place Order. ✅
⚠️ Key Rules
  • • When adding a new line: auth with last 4 of credit card (SSN alone won't cut it)
  • • Never enter DOB/SSN for anyone other than the primary account holder
  • • Smartwatch & tablet BYODs go through XM360/Retail360 or XOE

Mobile Returns & Exchanges

How to process a device return or swap in XM360 — with the right RMA type

▼
⏰ Time Window
14 Days
From delivery or in-store purchase
💰 Restocking Fee
$35
Waived if customer is deceased

🎯 Pick the Right Return Type

TypeUse When
Terminal ReturnCustomer wants to return device AND cancel their line
Buyer's Remorse ExchangeKeeps line, wants a different financed device (DPP)
BYOD ExchangeKeeps line, wants to swap for their own paid-off device
Warranty ExchangeDevice is defective (not damaged) — within 14 days only

✅ Before You Start

→
Only Account Holder, Primary, or Manager Xfinity ID can do returns/exchanges
→
Search account in XM360 → Lines of Service → confirm MDN and IMEI (use Qelp tool)
→
Run diagnostics + ITG in Einstein 360 to confirm exchange eligibility
→
Device must have no damage — liquid, jailbreak, unauthorized repairs = fees may apply
→
Ask customer to remove personal data. Apple: Find My must be OFF

📦 Process a Return

1
XM360 → Orders & Returns → Start a return or exchange
2
Pick device → Continue (one item per return)
3
Select Return Type & Return Reason → Next
4
Answer condition questions → Continue → Review Return Summary
5
Submit return — tell customer refunds take 3–5 business days

🔁 Process an Exchange

1
Same flow as return through Step 3
2
Care only: send iGuard link for customer's DOB & SSN (expires in 5 min)
3
Answer condition questions → Continue → Order review
4
Review Due today, next bill, monthly estimate with customer
5
Get agreement → Submit ✅
⚠️ Important Rules
  • • Devices bought or delivered more than 60 days ago are NOT eligible
  • • If device is on a deactivated line → activate it first before starting RMA
  • • Trade-in device cannot be recovered once shipped

Premium Unlimited customers can upgrade their device up to 2x per year — here's how

▼
📱
Eligible Plan
Premium Unlimited only
🔁
How Often
2x per year
60-day wait between upgrades

Trade-in Wait
2 full billing cycles
Under Premium Unlimited plan

💡 Three Upgrade Types

DPP Elite Upgrade$400 off over 36 months

Trade in your financed device early. Remaining DPP balance gets wiped. You start a new 36-month DPP. Device must be Grade A or B.

✅ Stackable with service deals   ❌ Cannot stack with other device deals

BYOD/PIF Elite Upgrade$400 off over 36 months

No trade-in required. Customer keeps old device and buys a new one on DPP. Great for customers who want to keep their current phone.

✅ Stackable with service deals (unlimited line plans)

TIPs Elite UpgradeUp to $830 off over 36 months

Trade in a fully paid-off device (BYO or PIF). Device doesn't need to be on an existing line. Trade-in value varies by make/model.

❌ Cannot stack with other device deals

💬 Common Scenarios — What to Say

📞 Customer on Premium Unlimited with 12+ months left on DPP
"Great news — with Premium Unlimited you can upgrade your device twice every 12 months. Your current payments get wiped and you get a discount off a new device!"
📞 Customer on Unlimited (not Premium) wants to upgrade
"Have you heard about our Premium Unlimited plan? For just $10 more per month you get more data, more security, and Elite Upgrade — which lets you upgrade your device twice a year!"
📞 Customer worried about paying off old device
"Once you trade in your current device with Elite Upgrade, you're no longer responsible for those payments. You only owe on your new device — which even comes at a discounted rate!"
🔮 Early Upgrade (for Unlimited plan customers)
  • • Can upgrade once 50% of device balance is paid
  • • One-time fee required (waived if enrolled in XMC)
  • • As of March 5, 2026: tablets and watches are now eligible too
⚠️ Credits get forfeited if...

Customer downgrades from Premium Unlimited or disconnects services — any remaining Elite Upgrade monthly credits are gone.

What every LED color on the Xfinity modem/gateway means

▼
○
No Light
Gateway is OFF — check the power cable
●
White Steady
All Good ✅ — Operational after activation. Nothing to do.

Red Steady

Internet EssentialsIEPP

IE Partnership Program (IEPP)

When a school, non-profit, or community org is sponsoring a customer's internet

▼
How it works:
A sponsor (school, non-profit, healthcare org, etc.) gives the customer a sponsorship code. The sponsor pays the IE monthly charge so the customer's bill shows $0.00 for internet.
✅ What the sponsor covers
IE ($14.95) or IE Plus ($29.95) internet only.
Customer pays $0 for internet while sponsored.
❌ What the sponsor doesn't cover
Xfinity Mobile, TV, or any other services. Customer pays for those separately.

👨‍💼 Agent Notes

→
Customer with sponsorship code: apply at internetessentials.com or transfer to IE New Sales in OneCTI
→
Customer with code gets error during application: transfer to IE New Sales destination in OneCTI
⚠️
Never give IE New Sales number to customers without a sponsorship code
→
Customer has code but blocked due to bad debt: call 1-844-963-0178 (Tier 2 can override). Then email BOS-Collections-Escalations@comcast.com for an "upgrade hold"
→
Existing Xfinity Internet customers can also switch to IEPP if they have a valid sponsorship code
⚠️ Non-Payment Note
If the account is shut off for non-payment, IE service is also disconnected. Customer must bring account current to reconnect IE.

Internet EssentialsAmnesty

IE Amnesty Program

Old Xfinity debt can be forgiven so low-income customers can qualify for IE

▼
💚 The Deal
If a customer meets IE eligibility requirements but has old Xfinity debt that's more than 1 year old, that debt can be completely forgiven so they can get internet service.

✅ Requirements to Qualify

  • • Debt must be more than 1 year old (based on final disconnection date)
  • • Customer must be the owner of the past debt
  • • Must meet all other IE eligibility requirements
  • • No max debt amount — any amount qualifies

❌ Doesn't Apply To

  • • Xfinity Mobile debt — cannot be forgiven
  • • Debt less than 1 year old (still in active collections)
  • • Customers who already received amnesty once

📋 What Happens After Amnesty

✓Debt is forgiven — collections agencies are notified to stop contact
✓Customer gets an IE account if they meet all other qualifications
⚠️Customer cannot upgrade or add services for 1 year
⚠️Amnesty is a one-time deal — no second amnesty if they fall behind again
👁️ How to spot an amnesty account
A CRE pop-up will appear when you access the account showing the date the amnesty expires.
